About us

Welcome to KASHCLASSES

We are a team of engineers and educators who have come together to bring a change. Shift the waves. Change the paradigm. Ready the new generation for the world they are going to face. Having witnessed and suffered through the absence of tech sector in the valley, we have taken to the helm and we envision a change that will spark a chain reaction. Never shall the youth suffer because of lack of tech companies in valley, if we rise and create opportunities today.

01

Why Choose us

We teach science, technology, mathematics and communication like no other. Our approach of teaching STEM through projects and experiments, with the best academic educators, delivers guaranteed results.

02

Our Mission

To equip children with life skills that traditional education has failed to avail and To imbibe a coding consciousness into the education system of Kashmir.

03

Our vision

KashKoding is an initiative of a professional engineer cum developer team dedicated to add a positive transformation in the quality of education in Kashmir.
